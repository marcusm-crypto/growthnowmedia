# CLAUDE.md — GrowthNowMedia Project Context

## What this project is
A 5-page marketing website for GrowthNowMedia, a growth agency that helps med spas and wellness clinics book appointments and grow revenue. The site needs to convert, build credibility, and remove friction. The primary CTA across the site is "Book a Call."

## Who they serve
1. Existing med spa and wellness clinic owners who want more bookings and revenue (their core service customer)
2. Aspiring entrepreneurs who want to start their own med spa or wellness clinic (the Growth Advisory program — the conversion emphasis on this site)

## Key proof points
- Worked with 500+ top wellness clinics and med spas across America
- Specialize in the med spa / wellness vertical (not generalists)
- Growth Advisory is a consulting program for new clinic owners

## Pages to build (in priority order)
1. Home — hero, social proof, services overview, Growth Advisory pitch, results, FAQ, CTA
2. Growth Advisory — dedicated conversion page for the consulting program (most important page)
3. Services — what they do for existing clinics
4. About / Results — credibility wall: case studies, logos, numbers, story
5. Contact / Book a Call — embedded calendar + contact form fallback
